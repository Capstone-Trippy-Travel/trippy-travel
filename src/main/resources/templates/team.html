The Trippi Travel Story

The idea behind Trippi Travel was to create a travel planning application that simplifies the way we plan and coordinate group travel. Planning can sometimes be time consuming, especially when it comes to travel to a foreign location. Trippi Travel sought to make that process easier by having a central location that allows users to plan and coordinate travel and gives users a shared experience by using a group consisting of one or more individuals. We have utilized a few technologies to create the functionality to do so and have created a platform that allows users to also share their travel experiences.
