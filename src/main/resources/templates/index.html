Trippy travel makes travelling fun by eliminating the hustle involved in planning and coordinating travel between friends and family. The app makes collaboration on trip ideas fun and seamless. Users may create trips, receive input on activities and plans from other trip collaborators. The collaborators can agree on events and activities for the trip, making the planning process exciting. Users can also peek into other trips to get ideas on what to include on their trips. The comments provided on past trips and experiences gives valuable feedback to other users looking to do the same. The app also allows users to share photos from past trips which brings a social-networking aspect to travelling. Bring the fun to your trip with Trippy travel.
